The Game Theory Matrix

Nash Equilibrium

No player can improve their outcome by unilaterally changing strategy. Markets find Nash equilibria constantly. Most traders don't know they're already in one.

The Fold Operator (F)

In the TO/TOGT framework, F is the commitment point — the moment at which rank-1 Jacobian loss occurs and the trajectory becomes irreversible. Every trade entry is an F event.

Dominant Strategy

A strategy that is optimal regardless of what others do. Holding THEORY is not a dominant strategy. That's the point. Dominant strategies have no upside.
