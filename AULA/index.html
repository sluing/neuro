Who Is Behind This

Researcher · Teacher · Builder

Pablo Nogueira Grossi is an independent researcher operating under the TOTOGT/Principia Orthogona programme through G6 LLC, based in Newark, NJ. He holds degrees in finance and economics from Baruch College and CUNY, and has a background in international banking at JPMorgan and Banco do Brasil.

He teaches English at UCEDA School of Elizabeth and at Hour House — his own learning programme at 229 Ballantine Pkwy, Newark, NJ — where every lesson is built on the same research he publishes internationally. He is bilingual in English and Portuguese.
