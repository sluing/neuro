Trading Rules

Rule Details
Risk Per Trade Maximum 0.75% of total account at risk per trade
Stop Loss MANDATORY — Always use ATR-based or percentage trailing stop
Profit Targets Guidance levels only (T1, T2, T3). You may scale out or let trailing stop run.
Daily Loss Limit Stop all trading if account is down -2% for the day
Entry Style Trend-following pullbacks (price above 50 EMA + pullback to support)
Trading Hours Regular market hours only (9:30 AM – 4:00 PM ET)
